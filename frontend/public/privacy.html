Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Studio Agents AI ("we", "us", "our") operates the Studio Agents mobile application and web platform. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address, display name, and authentication provider (Google, Apple, or email/password) when you create an account.
  • Usage Data: Feature usage analytics, session duration, and interaction patterns to improve the app experience.
  • User Content: Lyrics, audio files, images, videos, and other creative content you generate or upload within the platform.
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address for security and performance optimization.
  • Payment Data: Subscription tier and transaction history. Payment card details are processed securely by Stripe or Apple and are never stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Studio Agents platform and AI services.
  • To process subscriptions, credit purchases, and manage your account.
  • To send transactional communications (account verification, payment receipts).
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our AI agents and features.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, and Cloud Storage). All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption. We implement industry-standard security measures including token-based authentication, rate limiting, and input validation.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database, and file storage.
  • Google Gemini: AI text generation for lyrics, descriptions, and creative content.
  • Replicate: AI audio generation (MusicGen), image generation (Flux), and speech synthesis.
  • ElevenLabs: Premium voice synthesis and voice cloning.
  • Stripe: Payment processing for web subscriptions and credit purchases.
  • Apple StoreKit: In-app purchases and subscriptions on iOS devices.
  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics (with App Tracking Transparency consent on iOS).

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data at any time.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Export your creative projects and generated content.
  • Opt-out: Decline analytics tracking without affecting app functionality.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@studioagentsai.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data and content for as long as your account is active. Upon deletion request, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymized analytics data may be retained for service improvement.

7. Children's Privacy

Studio Agents is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us for removal.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests:
