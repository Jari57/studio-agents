# AI Agent Info Modal Content

## CrateDigger
**Description:** Discover rare samples and production inspiration across decades of music history.

**Features:**
- Voice search input
- Text-to-speech sample descriptions
- Decade filtering (60s-2020s)
- BPM and key information
- Production technique suggestions
- Free tier: 3 searches per session

**How to Use:**
1. Enter artist name, genre, or mood
2. Click SEARCH or use voice input
3. Browse curated sample suggestions
4. Use SPEAK to hear descriptions
5. Note BPM/key for your DAW

**Pro Tips:**
- Search by mood or emotion
- Try obscure genres for unique sounds
- Combine different eras
- Look for samples with clear breaks
- Research original artists for clearance
